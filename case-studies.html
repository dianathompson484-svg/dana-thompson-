The Scholar's Dilemma

Academic Press Author — Rachmaninoff Biography

The Challenge

A 398-page meticulously researched biography, the first in English to present Rachmaninoff in the fullness of his Russian Orthodox identity and faith. Endorsed by top scholars. Published by a respected academic press. And completely invisible to the music lovers, church musicians, and Russian culture enthusiasts who would treasure it.

What We Did

Repositioned the Kindle edition front and center. Targeted Orthodox Christian bookstores, classical music forums, Rachmaninoff fan communities, and Russian cultural organizations. Positioned the book for university course adoption in music history, Russian studies, and religious studies programs—creating steady, long-term visibility instead of a one-time spike.

The Result

The book moved from a "library purchase only" $150 hardcover to a discovered resource for exactly the readers who value its unique angle: the Orthodox soul behind the music.

The Sequel Cliff

Rachel Schneider — Fire & Metal Duology

The Challenge

Metal Slinger exploded. 44,577 ratings, 4.5 stars, 148,000+ Goodreads ratings. Readers called it the best romantasy of the summer. They stayed up all night reading it. The twist generated genuine word-of-mouth. Then Book Two released—and almost nobody found it. Three ratings. The readers who loved Book One weren't being served Book Two.

What We Did

Diagnosed the discoverability break between series installments. Optimized the series page to create a natural funnel. Targeted romantasy communities with the completed duology message. Activated the existing fan base with strategic review campaigns for Book Two.

The Result

The cliff between Book One's explosion and Book Two's quiet landing began to close. Readers who loved Metal Slinger finally discovered the conclusion they'd been waiting for.

The Invisible Ministry

Dr. Prayer M. Madueke — Deliverance by Fire Series

The Challenge

Over 100 books published. Decades of international ministry. A 740-page deliverance manual packed with practical teaching on breaking spiritual strongholds. Direct contact lines—website, email, active social media. And yet the flagship title sat at #4,631,874 with only 12 ratings. The content was life-changing. The discoverability was nonexistent. The catalog was so large it was fragmenting rather than amplifying his reach.

What We Did

Designed a reader journey across the catalog so each book led naturally to the next. Optimized the author page to immediately communicate authority. Positioned key titles against high-volume search terms in spiritual warfare and deliverance. Mobilized the existing ministry audience for honest reviews and engagement.

The Result

100+ books became a unified funnel instead of a scattered catalog. Readers searching for deliverance resources began finding a voice with decades of experience—instead of whoever had the best keywords.
